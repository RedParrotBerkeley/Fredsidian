# Example Vault Structure

```text
ObsidianVault/
  Assistant/
    Drafts/
    Briefs/
    Procedures/
  Projects/
  People/
  Decisions/
  Research/
  Reference/
  Daily/
```

## Suggested Meanings

- `Assistant/Drafts/`: assistant-prepared drafts for review
- `Assistant/Briefs/`: concise operational summaries
- `Assistant/Procedures/`: stable workflows and runbooks
- `Projects/`: project home notes
- `People/`: stable person/entity pages
- `Decisions/`: dated decision records
- `Research/`: topic summaries and source synthesis
- `Reference/`: durable evergreen material
- `Daily/`: dated logs and chronology
